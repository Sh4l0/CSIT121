Reflection

I think that this project was definitely the most difficult and time-consuming. However, the result feels extremely rewarding. I think the biggest challenge I faced was trying to maintain the structural styling of each page while keeping the aesthetic the same. Through hours of trial and error, alongside some YouTube videos and Google, I was able to get to a point where I really like the styling I chose. This project also allowed me to trust myself more and implement new parts of the overall design without needing to consult an outside source


A major issue I faced was also the styling on different screen sizes. At first, I wanted to approach the adaptive styling with the same approach I used in lab 5, changing the colors and adjusting the sizing. However, in hopes of being more professional, I kept all changes purely structural to ensure the site can be navigated no matter what. This gave a more cohesive and clean user experience in my opinion, which was perfect for this assignment.


Overall, this class taught me so much about web standards and web design, and has made me feel so much more confident about doing web design. I plan on using everything I learn in my personal career and hope that I can continue to improve.
